about me

hii, i'm Silvia, but around here you can call me nanaqmi ✧ this is my little corner of the internet where i share my art, post my newest handmade necklaces, and gush about anime and all things cute. honestly, most days i'm just alone in my room making stuff or out shopping for cute little things — i make everything by hand with way too much love, so thank you for stopping by and supporting a small creator ✧ don't forget to feed my tamaweb pet, play the minigames, and sign my guestbook before you go!
